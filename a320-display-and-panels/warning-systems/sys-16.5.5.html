16.0 Warning Systems

EGPWS Control Panel

Landing Flap 3 Switch

ON - When the LDG FLAP 3 switch is selected ON, the ON light illuminates and inhibits the GPWS flap mode (“TOO LOW, FLAPS”) when flaps 3 is selected as the final flap setting. GPWS FLAP 3 is displayed on the landing memo.
