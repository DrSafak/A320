16.0 Warning Systems

Weather Radar and PWS Control Panel

PWS Alert Warning

PWS Alert Warning Takeoff and Landing is active below 1200 feet RA. The alert zone ahead of the airplane is 1/4 NM, and extends out to 3 NM while the airplane is on the ground, then reverts to 1.5 NM once airborne.

During takeoff PWS alert provides “WINDSHEAR AHEAD, WINDSHEAR AHEAD” voice alert, a red W/S AHEAD message on both PFDs, and a PWS symbol on both NDs.

During landing PWS alert provides “GO AROUND, WINDSHEAR AHEAD” voice alert, a red W/S AHEAD message on both PFDs, and a PWS symbol on both NDs.

NOTE
 

Reactive Windshear is computed by the FACs. Reactive windshear is active when below 1300 feet RA and the flaps are extended to CONF 1 or greater. The reactive windshear voice alert is "WINDSHEAR, WINDSHEAR, WINDSHEAR".
