3.0 Electrical and Lighting

Emergency Electrical Control Panel

RAT and Emergency Generator Manual On Switch

AUTO - The Ram Air Turbine extends automatically, provided AC bus 1 and 2 are not electrically powered and speed is above 100 knots. On earlier aircraft, the emergency generator is only powered when the landing gear is up. On newer aircraft the emergency generator is powered above 125 KIAS with the gear up or down. The emergency generator is hydraulically powered by the blue hydraulic system. The emergency generator is connected to the electrical system when electrical parameters are normal.

FAULT - The RAT and Emergency Generator Fault light illuminates red with a loss of AC buses 1 and 2 if: the emergency generator is not supplying electrical power and the nose gear is up.

ON - The RAT extends and couples the emergency generator.

WARNING
 

Ram Air Turbine extension occurs any time the Ram Air Turbine Manual ON switch is selected, since the switch is always powered.
