3.0 Electrical and Lighting

Electrical System Displays

Dual AC Bus Failure

A ram air turbine (RAT) automatically extends if both AC busses 1 and 2 lose electrical power above 100 knots; however, a minimum airspeed of 140 knots (on earlier A320s) is required to provide sufficient blue hydraulic pressure to operate the emergency generator.

On later A320s and all A319s, the emergency generator operates until touchdown (greater than 125 knots), regardless of landing gear position.

In this example a dual generator failure has occurred, the RAT is extended, and the emergency generator is operating. Electrical power is provided to the:

  • AC ESS BUS
  • AC ESS SHED BUS
  • DC ESS and DC ESS SHED busses through the ESS TR
