12.0 Landing Gear

Brakes and Accumulator Indicator

Brake Pressure Indication

YELLOW BRAKE PRESSURE INDICATION - The brake pressure indication displays the yellow hydraulic system pressure that is supplied to the left and right brakes. Normally the green system is providing hydraulic pressure for normal brakes. When the parking brake is set to ON, the yellow hydraulic system is providing parking brake pressure. Click on link to see Schematic Parking Brake On. When the barking brake is set to OFF, the pressure indication is zero pressure if the green system is supplying brake pressure.

ALTERNATE BRAKES - The yellow hydraulic system automatically provides alternate brakes when:

  • Green hydraulic pressure is insufficient
  • Electrical power has failed
  • The BSCU has failed, or
  • The A/SKID & N/W STRG switch is selected OFF

In the last three cases, alternate braking is available but without antiskid. When braking without antiskid, pedal pressure must be controlled to prevent wheel lockup and brake pressure limited to a maximum of 1000 PSI on the BRAKES and ACCU PRESS indicator.
