3.0 Electrical and Lighting

Electrical System Displays

Dual AC Bus Failure Speed 50

When the speed is below 50 knots IAS, the AC ESS bus is no longer powered and the CRTs are lost.

The DC ESS bus remains powered which provides communiction capability
