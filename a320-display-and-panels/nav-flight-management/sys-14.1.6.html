14.0 Flight Management System

ADIRS Control Panel

IR Mode Selectors

OFF - The OFF position deactivates the associated ADIRU. Attitude data reference (ADR) and inertial reference (IR) data are not available.

NAV - The NAV position provides full inertial data to airplane systems. (Normal mode of operation.)

ATT - The ATT position provides only attitude and heading data. Magnetic heading must be entered via the ADIRS control panel, and must be reset frequently (approximately every 10 minutes).

Attitude Data Reference is available in NAV or ATT positions.
