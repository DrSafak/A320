12.0 Landing Gear

Brakes and Accumulator Indicator

Accumulator Pressure Indication

ACCUMULATOR BRAKE PRESSURE - Displays the brake accumulator pressure for the yellow hydraulic system.

If both the green and yellow hydraulic systems fail, alternate braking is supplied by the brake accumulator only. The accumulator supplies pressure for at least seven full brake applications.
