8.0 Flight Instruments

Primary Flight Display Symbols

Stall Warning Speed

The VSW is defined by the top of a red and black strip along the speed scale. It represents the speed corresponding to the stall warning, as computed by the FACs. The VSW symbol is displayed on the PFD in alternate and direct law.
