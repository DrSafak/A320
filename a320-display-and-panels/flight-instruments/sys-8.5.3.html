8.0 Flight Instruments

Clocks

Digital Chronometer

CHR COUNTER - Displays the chronometer time in minutes and seconds (up to 59:59) and is controlled by the CHR and RST buttons.

RST BUTTON - Resets the chronometer display.

UTC COUNTER - Displays time in 24-hour format.

DATE/SET BUTTON - Displays date in place of time.

CHR BUTTON - Starts and stops the chronometer.

GPS (UTC Selector)

  • GPS - The internal time and date are synchronized to GPS time.
  • INT - The clock disregards GPS and displays internal time or date.

ET COUNTER - Displays the elapsed time.

RUN (ET Selector)

  • RUN - The elapsed time counter runs.
  • STP - The elapsed time counter stops.
  • RST - The elapsed time counter is blanked.
