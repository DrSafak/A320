10.0 Hydraulic System

Hydraulic Control Panel

Blue Electric Pump Switch

AUTO - The ELEC PUMP operates, if AC power is available when in flight or on the ground, if one engine is running

OFF - When the BLUE ELEC PUMP switch is selected to OFF, the electric-driven pump is de-energize and the OFF light illuminates white.

FAULT - The BLUE ELEC PUMP FAULT light illuminates amber for a Blue hydraulic system:

  • Reservoir low level
  • Reservoir overheat
  • Reservoir low air pressure
  • Pump low pressure (inhibited on the ground when engines are stopped)
  • Pump overheat

The BLUE ELEC PUMP FAULT light extinguishes when the BLUE ELEC PUMP switch is selected OFF (except during an overheat, when the light remains illuminated as long as the overheat is present).
