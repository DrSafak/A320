7.0 Flight Controls

Flight Controls

Flight Control Inputs

All flight control surfaces are hydraulically actuated via hydraulic jacks. Flight control inputs are normally accomplished via electrical inputs from flight control computers to hydraulically actuated flight surfaces.

The side stick and rudder pedals input commands to the flight control computers. The flight computers then send appropriate electrical signals to hydraulic jacks.
