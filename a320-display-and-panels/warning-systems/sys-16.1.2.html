16.0 Warning Systems

ECAM Control Panel

 

Emergency Cancel Button

The EMER CANC button when pushed:

  • Present aural warning is cancelled
  • The MASTER WARN light switches extinguish
  • The message displayed on the ECAM is not affected
  • Present caution (single chime, MASTER CAUT lights, ECAM message) is cancelled for remainder of flight
  • The STATUS page is automatically displayed
  • The CANCELLED CAUTION message and the title of the inhibited failure appear on the status page. The cancelled caution inhibition is suppressed after AC power application until first engine start, but can be manually recalled by pushing RCL button for more than 3 sec
NOTE
 
Only spurious MASTER cautions should be cancelled.
