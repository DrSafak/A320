7.0 Flight Controls

Flight Control Laws

Alternate Law - Stall Warning Speed

It is important to recognize that you can stall and overspeed the aircraft in alternate law.

The VSW is defined by the top of a red and black strip along the speed scale. It represents the speed corresponding to the stall warning, as computed by the FACs.

The VSW symbol is displayed on the PFD in alternate and direct law.
