13.0 Navigation Auto Flight

AP/FD Vertical Mode FMAs

FLARE

The FLARE annunciation displays when FLARE mode engages at 40 feet AGL during an autoland ILS approach. The autopilot begins the flare maneuver at 30 feet and the thrust retards to idle. The pilot must manually retard the throttles after landing.

If the FLARE annunciation is not displayed by 30 feet, then the airplane cannot autoland and the pilot must execute a go-around.
