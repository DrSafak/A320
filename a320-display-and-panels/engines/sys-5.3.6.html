5.0 Engines and APU

Engine Warning and System Displays

Engine System Display 2

4. OIL TEMPERATURE INDICATION
Displays oil temperature in °C. The indication pulses if oil temperature increases above 156°C and stops pulsing when oil temperature decreases below 150°C. It becomes amber if the temperature exceeds 156°C for more than 15 minutes, or instantaneously when above 165°C.

5. START VALVE INDICATION - Displays position of start valve. A horizontal line through the circle signifies the valve is fully open. A vertical line signifies the valve is fully closed.

6. IGN INDICATION - Displayed during start sequence. The selected igniters A or B or AB are displayed when supplied.

7. ENGINE BLEED PRESSURE INDICATION
Displays bleed pressure upstream from the precooler. The value becomes amber below 21 PSI when N2 exceeds 10‰ with the engine start valve not closed, or when bleed over pressure is detected.
