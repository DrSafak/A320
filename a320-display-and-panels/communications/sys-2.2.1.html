2.0 Communications

Calls Panel

Emergency Call Switch

The calls panel allows the cockpit to initiate calls to the flight attendants via the cabin interphone system and the mechanic via the flight interphone system. The flight interphone system provides communication between the cockpit and the external service interphone panel located at the nose gear. The cabin interphone system provides communication between the cockpit, flight attendant stations, and various external locations on the aircraft.

RESET - Reset is achieved when the flight attendant hangs up the handset or by cycling the emergency call switch from ON to OFF.

ON - When the EMER Call Switch is selected ON:

  • The ON light flashes
  • The CALL light flashes
  • A pink light flashes at all flight attendant area call panels
  • An "EMERGENCY CALL" message appears on all flight attendant indication panels
  • A high/low chime sounds through all loudspeakers.
