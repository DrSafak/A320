4.0 Emergency Equipment

Emergency Exits and Slides

Over Wing Slides

OVER WING EMERGENCY EXITS - A slide is mounted in the fuselage at the trailing edge of each wing and is always armed.

If an over wing emergency exit is opened from either the inside or outside, the slide deploys.

The slides also contain a manual inflation handle for use in case the slide does not automatically inflate.
