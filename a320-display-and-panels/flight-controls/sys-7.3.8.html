7.0 Flight Controls

Flight Control Laws

Normal Law - Ground Mode

The ground mode is active when the aircraft is on the ground and until 50 feet radio altitude. Flight controls are in direct law. Bank angle protection is displayed after engine start. Trim can be manually set prior to takeoff when in the ground mode.
