7.0 Flight Controls

Flight Control Laws

Abnormal Attitude Law - Normal Limits

When the airplane returns to normal attitude limits from an extreme attitude limit, the following laws are active for the remainder of the flight:

  • Pitch alternate with load factor protection and automatic trim (load factor demand mode). No high nor low speed stability protections are available.
  • Roll direct
  • Yaw alternate

When the airplane returns to normal limits and pitch alternate law becomes active, it remains in alternate law until landing. There is no reversion to direct law when the landing gear extends.
