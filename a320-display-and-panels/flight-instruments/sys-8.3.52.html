8.0 Flight Instruments

Primary Flight Display Symbols

TCAS Fail Flag

When a TCAS system fault is detected, the TCAS Fail Flag (annunciation) appears in red on the PFD and ND.

In addition, a NAV TCAS FAULT message appears on the upper ECAM when a TCAS system fault is detected.
