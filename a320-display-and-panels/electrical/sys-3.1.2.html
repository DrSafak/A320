3.0 Electrical and Lighting

Electrical System Displays

External Power Available

The main AC system is divided into two electrical channels (1 and 2); they cannot be paralleled. Each AC bus is normally supplied by an IDG on the associated engine. The IDG incorporates a generator, a constant speed drive, and the drive oil system in a single unit. IDG oil is cooled by a fuel/oil heat exchanger. The IDG can be disconnected from an engine by an IDG disconnect switch, but can only be reconnected on the ground.

Engine Generators (IDGs) are providing power and external power is available in this example.
