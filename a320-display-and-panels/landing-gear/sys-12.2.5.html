12.0 Landing Gear

Landing Gear System Displays

Alternate Brake Mode

The ALTN BRK legend appears green if braking system is in the alternate mode. Alternate mode braking does not provide for auto braking. Antiskid may or may not be available depending on failures.

The yellow hydraulic system automatically provides alternate brakes when:

  • Green hydraulic pressure is insufficient
  • Electrical power has failed
  • BSCU has failed
  • A/SKID & N/W STRG switch is selected OFF

In the last three cases, alternate braking is available but without antiskid. If both the green and yellow hydraulic systems fail, the accumulator supplies pressure for at least seven full brake applications.
