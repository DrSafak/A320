12.0 Landing Gear

Landing Gear Panel

Auto Brake Switches

LO - Low may be selected for landing. If the deceleration rate from reverse thrust is not adequate, progressive pressure is applied to the brakes 4 seconds after ground spoiler deployment to provide the selected deceleration rate.

MAX - Maximum is normally selected for takeoff. During an aborted takeoff, maximum brake pressure is applied when ground spoiler deployment occurs.

MED - Medium may be selected for landing. If the deceleration rate from reverse thrust is not adequate, progressive pressure is applied to the brakes 2 seconds after ground spoiler deployment to provide the selected deceleration rate.

DECEL - The DECEL light illuminates green when actual airplane deceleration corresponds to 80% of the selected rate.

ON - The AUTO/BRK Switches arm the required deceleration rate. The ON light illuminates blue to indicate positive arming (LO, MED, or MAX).
