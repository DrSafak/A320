13.0 Navigation Auto Flight

Approach Capabilities

DH XXX

Displays the radio altitude minimums entered on the MCDU PERF APPR page (typically used for CAT II/III approaches). Only displayed during the descent and approach phases of flight.

For CAT III approaches, 100 feet is normally entered for the alert height.

The charted CAT II DH radio altitude is normally entered for CAT II approaches, but not less than 100 feet.
