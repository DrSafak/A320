8.0 Flight Instruments

Clocks

Chronometer ND Switches

CHRONO SWITCHES - A pilot's chronometer swtich is located above each ND on the glareshield. The first push of the the chronometer switch displays chronometer time in boxed format in the lower left corner of onside ND. The second push freezes the displayed value. The third push resets the chronometer and removes the display. The chronometer switch operates independently of other clocks.
